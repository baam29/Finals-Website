About

About Page

Welcome to my personal site!

This site is my personal manga & manhwa gallery, a place where I collect, rank, and share the series I have read and loved. It is not for reading full chapters, just a space to show off my favorites.

My Story

Why I build this site?

I started reading manga during high school, and it quickly became one of my favorite hobbies. I wanted a place to organize everything I’ve read and create a space to share my top picks.

Contents

Things you can do and see here:

  • My recent favorite manga’s and manhwa’s, either complete and ongoing.
  • Gallery of what I read and still reading. You can browse and check out new stories to read.
  • A ranking list, where I rank manga and manhwa based on their tier. This is good for recommending new manga and manhwa.
